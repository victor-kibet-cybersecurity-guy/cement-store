A Legacy of Strength

Founded in 1951, Bamburi Cement began operations in Mombasa with a single kiln and a bold vision to fuel Kenya's post-independence infrastructure boom. Over seven decades, we have grown into a powerhouse of innovation: from pioneering eco-friendly cement blends to championing circular economy models through our LafargeHolcim (now Holcim Group) affiliation.

Today, our state-of-the-art plant in Bamburi, Mombasa, produces world-class cements that have built iconic landmarks, housing projects, roads, and dams across East Africa. Our commitment to 'Green Cement' drives research into low-carbon cements, alternative fuels, and massive reforestation programs around quarries — turning exhausted mining sites into thriving ecological parks.

Committed to Net Zero by 2050

Through alternative fuels, carbon capture ready technologies, and reforestation, we are reducing our clinker factor and pioneering ECOPlanet® green cement range in the region. Our Bamburi quarry now hosts nature trails and wetlands — a model for industrial ecology.

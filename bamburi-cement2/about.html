A Legacy of Strength

Founded in 1951, Bamburi Cement began operations in Mombasa with a bold ambition to support Kenya's infrastructure and housing growth. Over the decades, the company has become a respected name in construction through dependable products and strong technical expertise.

From major roads and housing projects to industrial and commercial developments, Bamburi Cement has played a meaningful role in shaping the built environment of East Africa.

Building Kenya, one strong foundation at a time.

Bamburi Plant

The Bamburi plant in Mombasa stands as a key part of the company's history and production capability. It supports the supply of quality cement products used in homes, commercial structures, roads, and major infrastructure works.

This facility reflects Bamburi Cement's long-standing investment in reliable production, operational expertise, and continuous improvement for the Kenyan market.

  • Strategically located to support efficient supply and distribution.
  • Known for consistent product quality and dependable output.
  • Supports Bamburi Cement's commitment to sustainable industrial progress.

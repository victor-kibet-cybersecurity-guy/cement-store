Frequently Asked Questions

These are the latest frequently asked questions about our cement products, delivery, pricing, and ordering across Kenya.

What types of cement do you sell in Kenya?

We supply high-quality cement including Ordinary Portland Cement (OPC), Portland Pozzolana Cement (PPC), and specialized blends suitable for construction, plastering, and structural work.

What is the price of Bamburi cement in Kenya?

Current Bamburi cement prices in Kenya include: Bamburi Fundi (32.5) at KES 650, Bamburi Tembo (32.5) at KES 690, Bamburi Nguvu (32.5) at KES 740, Bamburi Powermax (42.5) at KES 950, Bamburi Duracem (42.5 MPa) at KES 1,050, Bamburi Powerplus (42.5 MPa) at KES 1,000, and Bamburi Powercrete (52.5 MPa) at KES 1,250. Prices may vary depending on location and quantity.

Do you offer delivery across Kenya?

Yes. We provide fast and reliable delivery countrywide including Nairobi, Kiambu, Nakuru, Eldoret, Kisumu and other regions.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery typically takes same day or next day within major towns. Remote areas may take 1-3 days depending on distance.

How can I place an order?

You can place an order by calling us, sending a WhatsApp message, or filling out the order form on our website.

Do you sell cement in bulk?

Yes. We supply both retail per bag and bulk orders for contractors, developers and hardware stores.

How many bags of cement do I need for my project?

This depends on your project size such as foundation, slab or plastering. Contact us with your details and we will help you estimate accurately.

Is your cement genuine Bamburi cement?

Yes. We only supply original, high-quality Bamburi cement sourced from trusted distributors.

What is the difference between OPC and PPC cement?

OPC sets faster and is ideal for structural works, while PPC is more durable and suitable for plastering and long-term strength.

Bamburi Cement Product Guide

Find the right Bamburi cement product for plastering, block work, structural concrete, precast jobs, aggressive environments, and specialist applications.

Choose the Right Product for the Job

This page gives a simple description of each Bamburi cement product so you can quickly understand what it is best used for. The descriptions are written in a practical way for site work, home building, masonry, structural concrete, and finishing tasks.

Masonry and plastering Structural concrete Special applications

Quick Notes

  • Use Fundi for smoother masonry and plastering work.
  • Choose Tembo, Nguvu, Powermax, or Power Plus where higher structural strength is needed.
  • Duracem, Powercrete, and Seti 300 serve more specialized site conditions and finishes.
General purpose

Bamburi Fundi

32.5R 50kg bag

Bamburi Fundi is made for masonry, plastering, screeding, and general finishing work where good workability and a smoother finish are important.

  • Suitable for block laying, walling, and rendering.
  • Helps masons achieve cleaner application and easier handling.
  • Commonly used for domestic and light commercial projects.
Structural work

Bamburi Tembo

42.5N 50kg bag

Bamburi Tembo is a stronger structural cement used where dependable performance is needed in beams, slabs, columns, and foundations.

  • Fits reinforced concrete and load-bearing work.
  • Useful for contractors handling larger structural elements.
  • Chosen where steady strength development matters.
High early strength

Bamburi Nguvu

42.5R 50kg bag

Bamburi Nguvu is designed for projects that need stronger early performance and quicker progress on site.

  • Useful for structural concrete and faster formwork cycles.
  • Suitable for demanding works where strength is a priority.
  • Helps reduce delays on fast-moving construction schedules.
High performance

Bamburi Powermax

52.5N 50kg bag

Powermax supports higher-performance concrete applications, especially where strong, durable output is required in commercial or precast work.

  • Good for heavy-duty structural and precast applications.
  • Supports stronger concrete mixes for demanding sites.
  • Often selected for projects with tighter performance targets.
Aggressive conditions

Bamburi Duracem

42.5N Sulphate resistant

Duracem is used where the concrete may face aggressive soil, chemical exposure, or marine-type conditions that need added durability.

  • Suitable for coastal works and difficult ground conditions.
  • Useful for foundations exposed to harsh environments.
  • Chosen where long-term durability is a key requirement.
Fast progress

Bamburi Power Plus

52.5R 50kg bag

Power Plus is intended for jobs that benefit from faster strength gain and reduced waiting time between construction stages.

  • Useful for fast-track site schedules and precast work.
  • Supports quicker handling on projects with tight timelines.
  • Suitable where early performance helps site productivity.
Concrete finish

Bamburi Powercrete

42.5R blended 50kg bag

Powercrete is designed for concrete work that needs dependable flow, reduced heat, and controlled performance in larger pours.

  • Suitable for mass concrete and general structural casting.
  • Useful where better handling in the mix is important.
  • Helps support cleaner placement on larger concrete jobs.
Special application

Seti 300

32.5N 25kg bag

Seti 300 is a specialized cementitious product commonly used for tile fixing and related finishing works rather than standard structural concrete.

  • Suitable for ceramic, terracotta, and natural stone applications.
  • Useful on internal walls and floors that require adhesive support.
  • Best treated as a specialist finishing product in the range.

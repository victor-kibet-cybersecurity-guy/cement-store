Frequently Asked Questions

Here are quick answers to the questions customers ask most often before ordering cement, paving blocks, or Kentanks from us.

Do you offer free delivery countrywide?

Yes. We offer free delivery countrywide on qualifying orders and help confirm the delivery arrangement before dispatch.

How long does delivery take?

Most deliveries are arranged within 24 hours depending on your location, stock availability, and order size.

How do I place an order?

You can place an order by calling us, sending a WhatsApp message, or contacting us through the details on the site for a quick quotation.

Do you sell only cement?

No. We also supply paving blocks and Kentanks in addition to our Bamburi cement range.

Which Bamburi cement products do you stock?

We stock products such as Fundi, Tembo, Nguvu, Powermax, Duracem, Power Plus, Powercrete, and Seti 300.

Can I order through WhatsApp?

Yes. WhatsApp is one of the fastest ways to order, request prices, confirm product availability, and share your location.

Do you supply products for both small and large projects?

Yes. We serve individual home builders, contractors, developers, hardware shops, and larger commercial projects.

Do you have cement prices per bag?

Yes. The website shows current prices for cement products, and you can contact us directly for bulk pricing and quotation support.

What sizes of Kentanks are available?

We currently list Kentanks from 1000 litres up to 10000 litres, with pricing available on the products page.

What is the price of paving blocks?

Our paving blocks are currently listed from KES 800 per square metre. You can reach out for larger quantity pricing and delivery arrangements.

Do you deliver outside Nairobi and Mombasa?

Yes. We deliver across Kenya and can guide you on the expected timeline based on your town or county.

What payment method do you use?

We guide customers on payment during order confirmation, and full payment is requested before delivery so we can reserve stock and process the order efficiently.

Can I ask for a quotation before ordering?

Yes. We can prepare a quotation based on the product, quantity, and delivery location you provide.

How can I know which cement type is right for my work?

You can contact us and we will help you choose the right product depending on whether your work is masonry, plastering, slab casting, structural concrete, or specialized applications.

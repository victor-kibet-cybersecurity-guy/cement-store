Your Privacy Matters

Privacy Policy

This page explains what information we may collect when you contact Bamburi Cement, how that information is used, and how you can request updates or removal of your personal details.

Information We Collect

We may collect the basic details you share when requesting a quote, placing an order, or contacting us for support. This can include your name, phone number, email address, delivery details, and any information you include in your message.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to respond to enquiries, prepare quotations, process orders, coordinate delivery, and offer customer support. We may also use contact details to follow up on an ongoing request you initiated with us.

Data Protection

We take reasonable steps to keep your information secure and limit access to people who need it for customer service, order handling, or delivery coordination.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. Information may only be shared when necessary to complete an order, support a delivery request, or comply with legal obligations.

Retention

We keep customer communication and order-related details only for as long as reasonably necessary to manage requests, provide support, and maintain basic business records.

Your Choices

You may contact us at any time if you want your information corrected, updated, or removed where applicable.

  • Request an update to your contact details.
  • Ask us to remove information you previously shared.
  • Contact us for clarification on how your details were used.

Quick Summary

We only use the information you share with us to help handle enquiries, orders, and delivery support.

  • No selling: Your personal data is not sold to third parties.
  • Limited use: We use details only for service and communication.
  • Your control: You can ask for correction or removal of your details.
